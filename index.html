About Me

I am an Assistant Professor and Teaching Learning Center AI Faculty Fellow in the Department of Engineering Technology at Sam Houston State University. I direct the Stochastic Simulation Lab, focused on AI for health, imaging, digital twins, cyber-physical systems, and immersive learning platforms. My research focuses on data-driven decision support, applied machine learning, AI-guided healthcare analytics, digital twins, and engineering education tools that help practitioners make better decisions.

Before joining SHSU, I was a Post-Doctoral Research Associate and Clinical Research Associate in the Department of Radiology at Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine. I completed my Ph.D. and M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Texas at San Antonio.
